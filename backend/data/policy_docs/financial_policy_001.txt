## Purchase Approval Requirements

All purchases must comply with the following approval thresholds:

### Tier 1: Under $1,000
- Department manager approval required
- Standard processing time: 1-2 business days
- Receipt must be submitted within 5 business days

### Tier 2: $1,000 - $10,000
- Department head approval required
- Finance department review mandatory
- Formal purchase order required
- Processing time: 3-5 business days

### Tier 3: $10,000 - $50,000
- VP-level approval required
- CFO review mandatory
- Board notification required
- Competitive bidding process recommended
- Processing time: 7-10 business days

### Tier 4: Over $50,000
- CEO and CFO approval required
- Board approval mandatory
- Formal RFP process required
- Legal review necessary
- Processing time: 14-21 business days

## Expense Categories

### Office Supplies
- Standard approval threshold: $500
- Bulk orders require competitive quotes
- Preferred vendors must be used when available

### Travel Expenses
- Pre-approval required for all business travel
- Economy class for flights under 5 hours
- Hotel accommodations: $200/night maximum
- Meal allowances: $75/day

### Software & Technology
- IT department approval required
- Security review mandatory
- Annual subscription limit: $5,000 per user

### Marketing & Advertising
- Marketing director approval required
- ROI tracking mandatory
- Campaign budgets require quarterly review

### Professional Services
- Consulting agreements require legal review
- Hourly rate caps apply by service category
- Statement of Work required for projects over $10,000

## Invoice Requirements

All invoices submitted for payment must include:

1. **Vendor Information**
   - Legal business name
   - Tax identification number
   - Complete contact information

2. **Invoice Details**
   - Unique invoice number
   - Invoice date
   - Payment terms
   - Due date

3. **Line Items**
   - Detailed description of goods/services
   - Quantity and unit price
   - Extended totals
   - Applicable tax calculations

4. **Supporting Documentation**
   - Purchase order number
   - Delivery confirmation (for goods)
   - Service completion certificate (for services)

## Tax Compliance

### Sales Tax
- Verify correct tax rate for jurisdiction
- Common rates: 5%, 7%, 10%, 18%, 20%
- Tax-exempt purchases require exemption certificate

### Value Added Tax (VAT)
- Standard VAT rates apply to applicable services
- VAT registration number must be verified
- Cross-border transactions require special handling

## Duplicate Payment Prevention

The following controls are in place to prevent duplicate payments:

1. Invoice numbers are checked against payment history
2. Vendor + Amount + Date combinations are flagged
3. Multiple invoices from same vendor on same day trigger review
4. System automatically holds suspected duplicates

---

## Fraud Prevention Measures

### Red Flags

The following patterns may indicate fraudulent activity:

- **Round Number Amounts**: Invoices for exactly $1,000, $5,000, etc.
- **Just Below Thresholds**: Amounts suspiciously close to approval limits
- **Vendor Name Variations**: Similar vendor names with slight differences
- **Missing Documentation**: Lack of purchase orders or supporting docs
- **Unusual Payment Terms**: Requests for immediate payment
- **Weekend Dates**: Invoices dated on weekends or holidays

### Required Actions

When fraud indicators are detected:
1. Hold payment immediately
2. Contact vendor for verification
3. Review historical transactions with vendor
4. Obtain manager approval before proceeding
5. Document all verification steps

## Vendor Management

### Approved Vendor List
- All vendors must complete onboarding process
- W-9 or equivalent tax forms required
- Background checks for vendors over $50,000 annually
- Annual vendor performance review

### Vendor Changes
- Banking information changes require phone verification
- Email address changes must be confirmed
- Physical address changes require documentation

---

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- Net 30 days from invoice date
- 2% discount for payment within 10 days
- Late payment fees: 1.5% per month

### Early Payment
- Approved for strategic vendors only
- CFO authorization required
- Must provide financial benefit to company

---

## Compliance & Audit

### Record Retention
- Invoices and supporting documents: 7 years
- Contracts and agreements: Life of contract + 7 years
- Tax records: Per IRS requirements

### Internal Audit
- Quarterly expense sampling
- Annual vendor audit
- Monthly reconciliation of accounts payable

### External Audit
- Full cooperation required
- Documentation must be readily available
- Findings must be addressed within 30 days

---

## Policy Violations

### Minor Violations
- Missing documentation: Warning + correction required
- Late submission: Delayed payment

### Major Violations
- Unauthorized purchases: Disciplinary action
- Fraudulent submissions: Termination + legal action
- Policy circumvention: Management review
