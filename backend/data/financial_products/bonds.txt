# Bonds - Fixed Income Investment Guide

## US Treasury Bonds
**Description**: Government-issued debt securities, considered safest investment
**Yield**: 3-5% annually (current rates)
**Risk Level**: Very Low
**Time Horizon**: 1-30 years (depending on maturity)
**Minimum Investment**: $100 (TreasuryDirect.gov)
**Tax**: Exempt from state and local taxes

**Types**:
- T-Bills: Under 1 year maturity
- T-Notes: 2-10 year maturity
- T-Bonds: 20-30 year maturity

**Recommended For**:
- Capital preservation
- Low-risk portion of portfolio
- Near-term goals (1-3 years)
- Retirees seeking steady income

---

## Corporate Bonds
**Description**: Debt issued by companies to raise capital
**Yield**: 4-7% annually (investment grade)
**Risk Level**: Low to Moderate
**Time Horizon**: 1-10 years
**Credit Ratings**: AAA to BBB (investment grade)

**Recommended For**:
- Higher yield than Treasury bonds
- Moderate risk tolerance
- Income-focused investors
- Diversification from stocks

---

## Bond Index Funds
**Description**: Diversified portfolio of bonds (government + corporate)
**Yield**: 3-5% annually
**Risk Level**: Low to Moderate
**Time Horizon**: 3+ years
**Fees**: 0.04% - 0.15%

**Popular Funds**:
- Vanguard Total Bond Market (BND)
- iShares Core US Aggregate Bond (AGG)

**Recommended For**:
- Easy bond diversification
- Lower risk than stocks
- Balancing stock-heavy portfolios
- Investors seeking steady income

---

## I Bonds (Inflation-Protected)
**Description**: US Savings Bonds adjusted for inflation
**Yield**: Fixed rate + inflation adjustment (currently 5-7%)
**Risk Level**: Very Low
**Time Horizon**: 1-30 years (must hold 1 year minimum)
**Purchase Limit**: $10,000 per person per year
**Tax**: Deferred until redemption

**Recommended For**:
- Inflation protection
- Emergency fund alternative
- Short to medium-term savings
- Risk-averse investors

---

## Municipal Bonds
**Description**: State/local government bonds, tax-advantaged
**Yield**: 3-4% (tax-free)
**Risk Level**: Low to Moderate
**Time Horizon**: 5-20 years
**Tax**: Federal tax-free, often state tax-free

**Recommended For**:
- High-income earners (higher tax brackets)
- Tax-advantaged income
- Supporting local communities
